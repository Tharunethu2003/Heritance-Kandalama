    Heritance Kandalama is a truly enchanting destination for weddings, where your special day is woven into the fabric of Sri Lanka's natural beauty and rich history. Nestled amidst the lush greenery of the Kandalama jungle and overlooking the serene Kandalama Reservoir, this unique and eco-friendly resort offers a picturesque setting for your wedding. Imagine exchanging vows with the mesmerizing backdrop of ancient rock formations and dense forests. Heritance Kandalama's expert event planning team takes care of every detail, from arranging stunning outdoor ceremonies to crafting sumptuous banquets infused with local flavors. With a blend of modern amenities and eco-conscious practices, Heritance Kandalama ensures that your wedding is not only a celebration of love but also a harmonious tribute to the surrounding environment. Create unforgettable memories in this breathtaking setting where nature's beauty harmoniously complements your love story.
