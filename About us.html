    • ABOUT OUR EXOTIC LUXURY HOTELS IN SRI LANKA

    The Heritance brand enriches destinations in Sri Lanka and overseas, bringing into focus the inheritance and culture of each location. Belonging to Aitken Spence Hotels, Heritance Hotels and Resorts is part of the chain of twenty two hotels and resorts that are scattered across South Asia and the Middle East, in Sri Lanka, India, the Maldives, and Oman.

    Heritance Hotels and Resorts is a celebration of traditional Sri Lankan heritage and hospitality with pioneer roots in sustainable tourism. The brand has successfully incorporated the convenience of location with the natural wonders in the vicinity, focusing on luxury, quality service and nurturing the local community.

    These concepts shine through the design and décor throughout the properties, inspiring guests to explore Sri Lanka through a unique perspective and create lasting memories coloured by the people, places and the stories that they discover.

    Open the door to new encounters and indulge in enchanting new journeys with Heritance

    Heritance Kandalama is an example of blend of Sri Lankan heritage, inherits and wild life. It is a luxurious 5 star eco friendly hotel, superiorly architected by world famous Geffory Bava. Heritance Kandalma is the first best Eco conceptual hotel in Sri Lanka and only a few in Asia.

    Kandalama hotel client base is eco friendly travelers who are looking for mouth watering food extravaganza, serene privacy, luxury and unique experiences. It attracts vast and reputed client tail by providing unique splendid luxury facilities and services. It has a wide European market for the majority with guest also visiting from the rest of the world. Nature lovers, eco friendly business travelers, honeymooners, families with kids, adventures, cultural and leisure tourists are main visitors of them.

    Envisioned and created by architect Geoffrey Bawa

    An unparalleled portfolio of impeccably designed hotels in destinations rich in heritage, inheritance and natural beauty. From golden beaches to emerald hills, the Heritance experience awaits guests with an eager embrace of warmth and hospitality.
